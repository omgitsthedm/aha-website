## Design Context

### Users

Design-conscious shoppers browsing primarily on mobile. They need to understand the product, price, fit, production timing, shipping, returns, and checkout path quickly. The storefront must support discovery without slowing or obscuring commerce.

### Brand Personality

Independent, exact, and restless. The only corporate identity shown is the full name `After Hours Agenda`. Do not use AHA monograms, mascots, sheep imagery, legacy logos, slogans, invented lore, or symbolic lockups as site branding. Product artwork may contain its original artwork when it is the merchandise being sold, but the surrounding interface must not repeat it as identity.

### Aesthetic Direction

Product-led Swiss/editorial utility with a dark, near-black theme, cool off-white typography, and one electric chartreuse signal color. Use a sharp, all-square geometry; a rigorous but asymmetric grid; large type that remains controlled; real product and design imagery sourced from the Desktop AHA client folder; and restrained transform/opacity motion. Avoid generic streetwear grunge, fake newsprint, faux subway systems, glassmorphism, gradient text, warm luxury beige, decorative logos, and templated card grids.

### Design Principles

1. The name is the identity. Set `After Hours Agenda` in type; never substitute a symbol or abbreviation.
2. The product is the visual. Use verified AHA design files and product mockups, not invented graphics or stock photography.
3. Commerce stays literal. Price, fit, production, shipping, returns, cart, and checkout language remain clear and calm.
4. One signal color, one radius rule, one dark theme. Use contrast and scale for hierarchy instead of ornamental effects.
5. Mobile is the primary canvas. Preserve 44px touch targets, visible focus, reduced motion, stable image ratios, and fast server-rendered pages.

### Design Dials

- `DESIGN_VARIANCE: 7` - asymmetric editorial composition without sacrificing shopping clarity.
- `MOTION_INTENSITY: 4` - one orchestrated entrance plus purposeful hover and state feedback.
- `VISUAL_DENSITY: 5` - enough product breadth for discovery, with deliberate breathing room.
