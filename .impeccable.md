## Design Context

### Users

After Hours Agenda serves shoppers discovering expressive, made-to-order apparel and returning customers managing an order. The storefront must move cleanly from discovery to product choice to a verified Square checkout on a phone, tablet, or desktop without hiding fit, production, shipping, or return details.

### Brand Personality

Independent, exact, expressive, and restless. After Hours Agenda is a New York label built around after-hours creative work and the black-sheep mark. The interface should feel graphic and authored without becoming noisy, precious, or less useful than the commerce it supports.

### Aesthetic Direction

Geometric editorial commerce: Swiss information discipline expressed through folded-paper geometry. Paper White is the ground; Ink Black carries the content; Coral Rose identifies action and selection. Sky, sage, and warm crease planes appear only as quiet fold cues. Use square edges, clipped corners, clear rules, asymmetric editorial composition, strong Poppins display type, and JetBrains Mono metadata.

### Design Principles

1. Commerce is literal. Price, variation, fit, production, shipping, returns, bag, and checkout remain explicit and calm.
2. Geometry has a job. Folds establish hierarchy or affordance; they are not decorative cards repeated around every block.
3. The black sheep is a signature, not wallpaper. Give it a few high-impact moments and preserve breathing room.
4. Motion explains hierarchy and state. Animate transform and opacity only, keep one orchestrated page entrance, and respect reduced motion.
5. Mobile is the purchase baseline. Preserve 44px targets, safe areas, explicit size selection, visible focus, stable imagery, and usable browser chrome across Safari/WebKit and Chromium.
6. Accessibility and speed are premium features. Maintain WCAG 2.2 AA contrast, semantic structure, keyboard paths, resilient storage/share fallbacks, and restrained client JavaScript.

### Design Dials

- `DESIGN_VARIANCE: 7` - asymmetric editorial compositions inside a disciplined grid.
- `MOTION_INTENSITY: 4` - purposeful entry, state, hover, and overlay motion; never constant spectacle.
- `VISUAL_DENSITY: 5` - product-rich and scannable, with deliberate pauses between shopping moments.
