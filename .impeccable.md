## Design Context

### Users

The public surface is temporarily a brand hold while the company rebuilds its identity, product presentation, and marketing system from zero. Existing customers may still use direct operational support routes such as order tracking, but no historical merchandise or collection structure should be promoted.

### Brand Personality

Independent, exact, and restless. The only corporate identity shown is the full name `After Hours Agenda`. Do not use AHA monograms, mascots, sheep imagery, legacy logos, slogans, invented lore, or symbolic lockups as site branding. Product artwork may contain its original artwork when it is the merchandise being sold, but the surrounding interface must not repeat it as identity.

### Aesthetic Direction

Temporary holding state: radical typographic restraint. Use the dark near-black theme, cool off-white type, all-square geometry, and only the full name `After Hours Agenda`. No product imagery, accent color, navigation, calls to action, newsletter capture, slogans, campaign language, or motion. The eventual storefront direction is intentionally undecided until the brand kit is approved.

### Design Principles

1. The name is the identity. Set `After Hours Agenda` in type; never substitute a symbol or abbreviation.
2. The reset is real. Do not surface historical products, collections, design files, or mockups as current marketing content.
3. Commerce stays literal. Price, fit, production, shipping, returns, cart, and checkout language remain clear and calm.
4. One signal color, one radius rule, one dark theme. Use contrast and scale for hierarchy instead of ornamental effects.
5. Mobile is the primary canvas. Preserve 44px touch targets, visible focus, reduced motion, stable image ratios, and fast server-rendered pages.

### Design Dials

- `DESIGN_VARIANCE: 4` - one deliberate typographic composition.
- `MOTION_INTENSITY: 1` - static until the new motion language is approved.
- `VISUAL_DENSITY: 1` - name only.
